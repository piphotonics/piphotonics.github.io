Our Vision

Pioneering the future of photonic design through AI innovation

Our Mission

At Pi Photonics, we're on a mission to transform the field of photonic integrated circuit design through the power of artificial intelligence. We believe that by combining cutting-edge AI research with deep domain expertise in photonics, we can unlock new possibilities in optical computing and communications.

Our Approach

We're building a team of world-class researchers and engineers who are passionate about pushing the boundaries of what's possible in photonic design. Our approach combines expertise in machine learning, photonics, and semiconductor manufacturing to develop innovative solutions that will shape the future of the industry.

Our Founder

Dr. Sunil Pai, our founder, brings a wealth of experience in quantum computing, photonics, and AI. With a PhD from Stanford University and experience at organizations like PsiQuantum and Google X, Dr. Pai is leading our efforts to build the next generation of photonic design tools.

At Google X, Dr. Pai worked on large-scale constrained optimizations for photonic inverse design, and at PsiQuantum, he contributed to various aspects of quantum photonic systems. This experience has given him unique insights into the challenges and opportunities in photonic design that we aim to address at Pi Photonics.

Our Research Focus

We're exploring how machine learning can transform photonic design automation, with a focus on developing novel algorithms that can suggest optimal photonic structures and configurations.

Future Goals

Our goal is to build AI tools that will make photonic design more accessible, enabling faster development cycles and more efficient photonic integrated circuits for applications in computing and communications.
